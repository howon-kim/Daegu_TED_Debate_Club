Constitution

Purpose of club

The purpose of the club is to improve listening and speaking skills to fluently speak English in an informal or formal situation. In addition, increase attention towards various TED topics and sharpen the critical thinking skill. The study group provides members opportunities to converse in English.

Membership

Members must have intermediate speaking and listening skills in English. There is no membership fee but there is the fee for booking the study room.

Duties of the members

  • Every communication must be English in Only-Engish session.
  • Every member must be called by their nickname or English name.
  • Age is treated as private part of every member, therefore, asking it treated as invading the personal area.

Meetings

  • Club meeting is held once a week on Friday at 10:00 AM.
  • Study always starts after 10 minutes of proposed time.
  • Meeting consists of two parts. TED debate and free talking.

Responsibilities

  • Every member has to prepare given TED lectures completely before the time of the club day.
  • Every member should not miss more than 2 meetings a month for any reason. Two consecutive absents may result in forfeiting membership
  • Every member should post TED topic on every Monday. Club members not following the due date may not participate the next meeting.
